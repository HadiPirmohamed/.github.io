Office Renovation

Freelance Work



An interior renovation project for a 7000 sq ft office space in Dubai. The project involved redesigning the space for maximum efficiency and creating an enjoyable working environment. The addition of spaces, positioning of rooms and extension of areas were designed for the increase in workforce and to better tackle the harsh summer climate of Dubai. The plan is divided in 3 sections, management (Right), workforce (Middle) and client meetings areas (Left).

Residential Loft Conversion

Freelance Work



An interior renovation and loft conversion for a new-build detached house in Watford. The project consisted of designing a new study, an extended living/dining room, an additional bedroom, en-suite and extra storage space. This was achieved through creating an open plan layout on the ground floor to create an airy atmosphere whilst also allowing space for a 2 person study. Bedroom 3 was relocated to the loft to create space for a walk-in closet in the master bedroom and a staircase to access the loft. Finally, the loft consisted of bedroom 3 and another master bedroom with a walk-in closet and en-suite. All design considerations where made to ensure the project was affordable and within the clients budget.

Art & Design Studio

Foundation in Art and Design



Coventry University's Art & Design foundation studio was redesigned for the main purpose of creating an inspiring and uplifting environment for students to learn and design in. The studio space was split into thee sections, 2 open classrooms on either side of the space, and an area in the middle for group collaboration and discussion. Sufficient storage was also designed through the orange drawers and cupboards which were strategically placed around the room.
